About Us

    Our Background

    We are AutoServe Inc. We've been in existence for roughly 10 years. It was all started by Winston Fields at his home in Arima. His talent as an auto mechanic and his reputation for quality work at a fair price, and excellent customer service, saw his small garage grow to be a major player in the auto-maintenance industry in Trinidad and Tobago. Winston attributes his success to ensuring his mechanics are well trained and most importantly, not being afraid to innovate in an effort to improve productivity and hence customer satisfaction.
